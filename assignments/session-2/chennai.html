Chennai

About Chennai:

Chennai, the capital city of Tamil Nadu in South India, is a bustling urban center brimming with history, culture, and modernity. Formerly known as Madras, the city boasts a rich legacy dating back to ancient times, evident in its architectural marvels such as the Kapaleeshwarar Temple and Fort St. George. Chennai's long coastline along the Bay of Bengal offers respite from the urban hustle, with Marina Beach stretching for miles, attracting locals and tourists alike to its sandy shores and vibrant promenades.
